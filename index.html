FAQs



What is CANTIKXPERT? +
CANTIKXPERT is a digital platform that promotes awareness about safe and high-quality local Malaysian makeup brands.
We aim to educate users about cosmetic safety, NPRA registration, and empower you to glow safely and confidently!
Is CANTIKXPERT affiliated with any government body like NPRA or KKM? +
No, CANTIKXPERT is an independent awareness platform created for educational purposes.
We reference NPRA and KKM guidelines to help users make informed cosmetic choices.
What if I suspect a product is fake or harmful? +
Avoid using it immediately.
You can report suspicious products directly to NPRA through their website, or learn how to identify suspicious products through our Glow Sense page.
Does CANTIKXPERT sell any products? +
No. We do not sell or endorse any specific brands commercially.
Our goal is to educate, not profit.
